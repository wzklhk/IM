## Key Insights

### Fee Analysis
- 513100 (国泰) at 0.80% is **33% more expensive** than the 0.60% group
- All other 8 ETFs charge 0.60% total
- Difference: 国泰 charges an extra 0.20%/year = on ¥100k position, ¥200/year extra

### Tracking Error
- **Best**: 513300 (华夏) at 0.15% — significantly ahead of peers
- Worst: 159659 (招商) at ~0.35%, 159660 (工银) at ~0.30%
- Small-cap ETFs have wider tracking errors due to higher implementation costs

### Premium/Discount Risk
- 159941 (广发): Often trades at 0.3-0.5% premium due to QDII quota tightness
- 513300 (华夏): Most stable premium (0.1-0.2%) due to ample QDII quota
- Small ETFs (159660, 159659): Wild premium/discount swings — unreliable entry/exit

### QDII Quota Status
- **Most ample**: 华夏, 易方达
- **Most constrained**: 广发, 国泰, 招商
- Impact: Tight quota → fund suspends申购 → ETF can't create new shares → premium widens

## Recommendation

**For entering Nasdaq 100 for the first time (DCA):**
1. **513300 (华夏)** — Best overall: lowest tracking error, stable premium, good liquidity, ample QDII quota
2. **513110 (华安)** — Good backup if 513300 has liquidity issues
3. **159941 (广发)** — Only if liquidity is paramount (largest volume), but accept 0.3%+ premium cost on entry

**Do NOT buy 513100 (国泰)** — 0.80% fee is too high for long-term holding. If user already holds it (e.g., from earlier), sell with limit order and switch to 513300.
