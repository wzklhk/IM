## "出差优势" Effect

During overseas travel:
- Company covers accommodation/food → personal expenses near zero
- Full per-diem becomes savings → monthly savings can spike to 25K-30K
- Can open overseas brokerage → buy VOO/QQQ directly (no QDII limit, lower fees)
