## The Core Tension

> "存够100万就fire" — but 100万 is not enough for full retirement in China.
> 
> Real meaning: "100万 gives me the confidence to quit overseas delivery and do work I actually like."

## Coast FIRE Math

```
Assets:             ~80万
Target safety net:  100万 (need ~20万 more)
Time to target:     6-12 months (one more overseas project)

At 100万:
  4% withdrawal:   ¥40K/yr = ¥3,330/mo
  Couple living in Guangzhou: ~¥7K/mo rent + food + transport
  Deficit:         ¥3,670/mo — covered by new AI job salary
  AI job take-home: ~¥18-21K/mo (assuming ¥25-30w annual)
  After covering deficit: still saving ¥14-17K/mo
```

**Key insight:** The ¥100万 portfolio isn't "retirement money" — it's a safety cushion that makes a ¥10-15万 salary drop completely painless. The user can take a lower-paying AI role, enjoy the work, and still save every month.

## The "广积粮" Strategy

User's chosen path: one more overseas project (Nepal, ~6-12 months) to hit ¥100万, then transition.

```
Phase 1 (当前): Overseas project in 达卡/加德满都
  ├─ Save ¥20-30万 (hit ¥100万)
  ├─ Learn AI basics at 3-4 nights/week (Python, RAG, Agent)
  └─ 郝明智 (local agent) builds AI project framework

Phase 2 (12个月后): Return to Guangzhou
  ├─ ¥100万 portfolio generating ¥3-4K/mo
  ├─ AI project portfolio ready (RAG + Agent + Spring Boot wrapper)
  └─ Apply to Guangzhou/Shenzhen AI platform roles

Phase 3 (长期): Coast FIRE
  ├─ Works AI job he enjoys (not 996, not overseas)
  ├─ Portfolio grows (even ¥1-2K/mo contributions compound)
  └─ Can always walk away if dissatisfied — portfolio covers survival
```

## Why This Works for This Profile

1. **Overseas income accelerator**: savings rate jumps to 80%+ during foreign assignments
2. **Existing technical foundation transfers well**: K8s/OCP production experience → MLOps/AI infra (the user's chosen AI entry point is not algorithms, but platform engineering)
3. **Age 27**: optimal transition window — old enough to have real engineering experience, young enough to absorb new tech stack
4. **Undergrad/masters at Xidian (西电)**: strong alumni network in Guangzhou/Shenzhen tech companies
5. **"精神广东人"**: cultural affinity with target city reduces relocation friction
